Official GPA Calculation

GPA = Σ(GP × Credits) ÷ Σ Credits

Grade Conversion

  • 96–100 → GP = 4.00
  • 91–95 → GP increases from 3.58 to 3.92
  • 81–90 → GP increases from 2.75 to 3.50
  • 71–80 → GP increases from 1.92 to 2.67
  • 61–70 → GP increases from 1.08 to 1.83
  • 51–60 → GP = 1.00
  • 0–50 → GP = 0.00

Final GPA is rounded to one decimal place.
